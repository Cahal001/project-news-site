When I am knitting I feel at peace. I loose track of time, just like mindfulness. It's the best kind of therapy

Caroline Storm has found inner peace and built her life around it

She started knitting only a few years ago, and has never stopped since.
"I was going through a rough patch in my life, and needed something to calm me down. A friend suggested I try knitting, and it was love at first stitch" she says.

Caroline has made her passion for knitting into a business, and now designs patterns and sells yarn online. She has made the perfect combination of happiness and work, and is an inspiration to many.
"That way I never have the feeling of working" she says. Her company is called Storm Knits, and you can find her patterns on Ravelry and Etsy.
